Chicken & Broccoli Alfredo

Ingredients

  • 2 Chicken Breasts
  • 15 oz Alfredo sauce
  • 1 Bag of Frozen Broccoli
  • 1 tablespoon of salt
  • At least 1 cup of chicken broth
  • Noodles of your choice

Instructions

  1. Place chicken breasts in a crockpot
  2. Pour Chicken Broth into crockpot, enough to cover the chicken breasts
  3. Turn Crock Pot on high, let sit for 5 hours
  4. After chicken is done, drain the remaining liquid
  5. Prepare a pot of water half way full
  6. Add 1 tablespoon of salt to the water, and bring the water to a boil
  7. When boiling, add your noddles
  8. When noodles reach desired consistancy, drain the water and pour noodles into crockpot
  9. Pour Alfredo sauce into crockpot
  10. Follow directions from the bag of broccoli, to steam it
  11. When Broccoli is done, put it into the crockpot
  12. Mix together all the ingredients and enjoy!
