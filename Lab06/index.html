Whats the weather like in Wisconsin?

Wisconsin's weather changes dramatically throughout the year, moving through a range of distinct seasons. The cold months bring snow and freezing temperatures, particularly in the northern regions, often leading to harsh conditions and occasional snowstorms. As the year shifts, the chill begins to fade, and the landscape thaws, though the weather can still be unpredictable with surprise snowfalls and frequent rain showers. The warmer months arrive with sunshine and rising temperatures, offering a chance to enjoy outdoor activities, but also bringing occasional thunderstorms and a bit of humidity. As the year winds down, the air cools again, and the trees showcase brilliant colors before the frost starts to settle in. Each season flows into the next, bringing with it a new rhythm and feel to the landscape, making Wisconsin's climate one of constant change and contrast.
